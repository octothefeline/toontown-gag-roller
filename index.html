Toontown Gag Roller
by octothecat v1.0
Welcome to the Toontown Gag Roller! Let fate decide what gag you'll use! Click each gag on the gag panel to toggle them on and off. Disabled gag buttons won't be rolled. Use the checkboxes on the left to toggle the entire track. The buttons on either side of the toon and cog display can be used to add or remove one.
